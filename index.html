What is this place?

Hello! My name is Nick Kotte. I developed this website as a means of presenting my projects in an easy and accessible way. The main goal is to make each one as interactable as possible with the only limitations being the browser and the device in use. Along with the projects, I will have written informational blogs to explain the purpose it serves and how it was made.

On the other hand, I have projects that are not website compatible which, instead, will be available for download via the projects panel or my GitHub. So far, I have ported 2 small projects to serve as a proof of concept. More are in development and will release as soon as possible!
